Biography

Born in 1978, Sethrongkyu Sangtam is a committed leader from Longkhim, Tuensang District. An alumnus of Hindu College, Delhi University (BA History, 2000), he entered public life with the vision to empower Nagaland through innovation and tradition.

2000

Graduated with BA History from Hindu College, Delhi University

2008

Entered public service with focus on community development

2018

Elected as MLA for Longkhim Chare constituency

2023

Appointed as Advisor for IT & Evaluation

Key Achievements

Nagaland IT Park Initiative

Introduced comprehensive IT Park proposal to Union Government, paving the way for technological advancement in Nagaland.

Infrastructure Development

Successfully launched new PHE office in Longkhim, improving water supply and sanitation services for the community.

Digital Connectivity

Promoted 4G connectivity initiatives in tribal regions, bridging the digital divide and connecting remote communities.

Evaluation & Assessment

Released comprehensive state-level evaluation reports for welfare schemes, ensuring transparency and effectiveness.

Vision & Mission

Technology for all. Unity among tribes. Legacy of progress.

Building a modern Nagaland that honors its rich cultural heritage while embracing technological innovation. Our mission is to create inclusive development that benefits every community, bridging traditional wisdom with contemporary solutions.

Together, we can transform Nagaland into a beacon of progress, where technology serves humanity and tradition guides innovation. Our strength lies in our unity, our future in our collective vision.
